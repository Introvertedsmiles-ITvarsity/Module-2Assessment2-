SYNOPSIS

Synopsis: In this live-action prequel to "101 Dalmatians," young fashion designer Estella, who is destined to become the wicked Cruella de Vil, finds herself embroiled in London's punk rock revolution while trying to make a name for herself in the fashion world. As she becomes more daring, she catches the eye of Baroness von Hellman, a fashion legend who soon becomes her bitter rival. Through a series of events fueled by ambition and revenge, Estella embraces her darker side and transforms into the iconic villain we all know as Cruella.
